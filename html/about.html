FAQs:

Why do I get an error message when I try to use my repo?

Please verify that you typed the information in correctly. Remember, the repo must be public in order for us to pull its data!

Does this site collect any private information?

No. FroYoMyRepo only gathers public information that is freely available on GitHub. Once your FroYo is created, all data is forgotten (no persistent storage).

Are there any usage restrictions when using my FroYo image?

Nope. Feel free to add it to your README file in your repo, or use it an any other way. We'd appreciate it if you gave us credit, but it's not required.

Where can I buy the artwork assets used?

The art assets can be purchased here. (Note: We have no affiliation with the artist, other than the fact that we are a licenced user of his artwork. We are simply giving credit where it is due.)
